Latex Paint FAQs

  • What surfaces can I apply Calyco Paint to?
    Interior surfaces like plaster, drywall, brick, and previously painted walls. Limited outdoor use recommended.
  • Is the paint single-pack or two-pack?
    Single-pack for standard coatings.
  • How is the paint applied?
    Roller, brush, or airless spray depending on finish.
  • What’s the drying and recoating time?
    Touch dry in 30 min–2 hrs; recoat after 1–2 hrs at 20°C and low humidity.
  • What is the coverage per liter?
    10–12 m² per liter (1 coat).
  • How can I get new colors?
    Use 5–10% universal colorant compatible with base paint.
  • Why does the finish look patchy?
    Could be due to uneven absorbency, no primer, or over-brushing. Apply another even coat.
  • Can it be used in humid areas?
    Yes, but for heavy humidity/splashing, use Stain & Sealer along seams.
  • What is the expected durability?
    10+ years with proper prep; varies by usage and environment.

Stain & Sealer FAQs

  • Where should I use Calyco Stain & Sealer?
    On driveways, roofs, joints, windows, doors, masonry, wood, and more.
  • How to apply for a professional finish?
    Clean surface, stir well, apply evenly, and allow 24–48 hrs to cure.
  • Advantages of using it?
    Waterproofs, seals, resists mildew, UV, and harsh conditions.
  • How long does it last?
    5–10 years under normal conditions; 18 months under extreme exposure.
  • How long does it take to cure?
    Skins in 30 min, recoat in 2–4 hrs, full cure in 24–48 hrs.
  • How is it different from other brands?
    Offers durability, UV protection, moisture resistance, easy application.
  • Best temperature range for application?
    5°C to 40°C (41°F to 104°F); avoid freezing or heavy rain.
  • Is it mold and mildew resistant?
    Yes, has anti-microbial properties for damp areas.
  • Where should it not be used?
    Avoid oily surfaces and submerged areas like swimming pools.
