FAQs

What surfaces can I apply Calyco Paint to?
Suitable for plaster, drywall, brick, and previously painted surfaces. Indoor preferred.
Is the paint single-pack or two-pack?
Single-pack, ready to use for standard applications.
What’s the recommended drying and recoating time?
Touch dry in 30 mins to 2 hours. Recoatable after 1–2 hours.
Can I use it in high-humidity areas?
Yes. Use with sealer for best performance in bathrooms and kitchens.
How long does Calyco Sealant last?
Typically 5–10 years with proper preparation and application.
What surfaces should not be used with Calyco Sealer?
Avoid greasy surfaces or areas constantly submerged in water.
