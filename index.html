Latex Paint

Calyco Latex Paint offers superior coverage and exceptional durability for interior and exterior surfaces. Ideal for plaster, drywall, and wood with excellent protection against moisture and stains.

Stain & Sealer

Our Stain & Sealer provides long-lasting protection against stains, dirt, and moisture while offering a beautiful, glossy finish. Perfect for outdoor use.

Latex Paint FAQs

  • Where can I use it? Interior plaster, drywall, brick, painted walls.
  • Application? Roller, brush, or airless spray.
  • Dry time? Touch dry in 30 min–2 hrs, recoat after 1–2 hrs.
  • Coverage? 10–12 m² per liter (1 coat).
  • New colors? Use universal colorant (5–10%).
  • Patchy finish? Apply another even coat after primer.
  • For humid areas? Use Stain & Sealer on seams.
  • Durability? 10+ years with proper prep.

Stain & Sealer FAQs

  • Where to use? Driveways, roofs, joints, windows, masonry, etc.
  • Professional finish? Clean surface, stir, apply, 24–48 hrs cure.
  • Benefits? Waterproofs, seals, UV & mildew resistance.
  • Durability? 5–10 years (18 months extreme).
  • Different from others? UV protection, easy application.
  • Temperature? 5°C to 40°C ideal for application.
  • Mold resistant? Yes, anti-microbial.
  • Where NOT to use? Oily surfaces, swimming pools.
