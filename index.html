FAQs

What surfaces can I apply Calyco Paint to?
Suitable for interior applications on plaster, drywall, brick, and previously painted walls. It can be used outdoors but is not fully recommended for long-term exterior exposure.
Is the paint single-pack or two-pack?
Our paint is single-pack (for standard coatings).
How is the paint applied?
Using a roller, brush, or airless spray depending on desired finish.
What’s the recommended drying and recoating time for Calyco Paint?
Touch dry: 30 mins to 2 hours. Recoatable after 1–2 hours. Drying time may vary with humidity and temperature.
What is the coverage per liter or gallon?
Approximately 10–12 m² per liter (1 coat).
How can I get new colors for the paint?
Use universal colorants (5%–10% by volume) and mix thoroughly into the base paint.
I’ve applied Calyco Paint but the finish looks patchy—what went wrong?
Patchiness may result from uneven absorbency, lack of primer, or over-brushing. Apply another even coat on a dry, dust-free, primed surface.
Can Calyco Paint be used in high-humidity areas like kitchens and bathrooms?
Yes, it resists light moisture. Use Stain & Sealer along edges for extra protection.
What is the expected durability or lifespan of the paint?
Typically 10+ years depending on traffic, usage, prep, and environmental exposure.
Where should I use Calyco Stain & Sealer?
Ideal for driveways, roofs, windows, doors, concrete joints, bathrooms, kitchens, wood, asphalt, and masonry. Compatible with wood, glass, PVC, concrete, ceramic, metal, and plaster.
How do I achieve a clean, professional finish when applying Calyco Stain & Sealer?
Clean and dry the surface. Stir well. Apply with brush, roller, or spray. Smooth while wet. Wipe excess and allow 24–48 hrs to cure.
What are the advantages of using Calyco Stain & Sealer?
Waterproofing, crack prevention, insulation, corrosion protection, mildew resistance across various surfaces like driveways, roofs, bathrooms, etc.
How long does Calyco Stain & Sealer last once applied?
5–10 years with proper application. Under harsh conditions, degradation may begin in 18 months.
How long does it take to cure completely?
Skins in 30 mins, recoatable in 2–4 hrs. Full cure in 24–48 hrs. Up to 72 hrs in humid environments.
How is Calyco Stain & Sealer different from others?
Combines UV resistance, moisture barrier, and easy application. Comparable to Penofin, Minwax, Spar Urethane.
What are the best temperatures for applying Calyco Sealant?
Between 5°C and 40°C. Avoid freezing or rainy conditions for proper curing.
Is Calyco Stain & Sealer resistant to mold and mildew?
Yes, it's formulated with anti-microbial agents to resist mold in humid areas.
What surfaces should it NOT be used on?
Avoid oily, greasy surfaces and areas under constant water immersion like swimming pools.
